Financial Tools

Financial Dashboard

See your operating balance, reserve fund, and monthly spending at a glance — no accounting degree required.

Dues Tracking & Late Fees

Track who's paid, who's late, and who's delinquent. Automate late fees and payment reminders so you don't have to.

Online Payments

Accept ACH and card payments directly through the platform. Funds deposit straight to your HOA bank account.

Expense Tracking

Log landscaping, repairs, insurance, and vendor payments. Attach receipts and keep everything organized year-round.

Board Reports & Audit Trail

Auto-generate a clean monthly financial summary — ready to present at your next board meeting in one click.

Community & Operations

Homeowner Portal

Let residents check their dues balance, make payments, and stay current on community news — all in one place.

Violations & Compliance

Log violations, send notice letters, and track resolution status — all with a documented paper trail.

Work Orders & Maintenance

Residents submit requests, the board assigns and tracks them through to done. No more lost email threads.

Architectural Review (ARC)

Homeowners submit modification requests online; the board reviews and approves or denies with a clear record.

Online Voting & Elections

Run board elections and community votes digitally, with results tallied automatically and stored for the record.

Meetings, Calendar & Announcements

Schedule meetings, post minutes, and share community-wide announcements — all in one shared calendar.

Documents, Directory & Vendors

Store governing documents and vendor contracts, and keep a searchable resident directory — all in one vault.
